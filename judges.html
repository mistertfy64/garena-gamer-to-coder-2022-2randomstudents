Judges

Symmetrica

Judge of Symmetrical Bodies

Symmetrica was born into a perfectly symmetrical environment. That means that everything she sees is perfectly aligned and mirrored. After seeing so many symmetrical objects, she decided to do the same with her body. The Higher Judges acknowledged her efforts to match the environment, and therefore endorsed her as a Judge.

Zepto

The Young Overachiever Judge

In contrast to having his name derived from the number 10-21, the young Zepto has achieved so much more than any of us ever will in our lifetime when he was 7. Due to his unbelievably high intelligence and talent, it didn't take long for the Judges to spy and eventually endorse him.

Dracomini

The Dragon Judge

Ever since he was a child, Dracomini trained himself 20 hours per day riding a dragon. It didn't take long for pedestrians and neighbors to notice. With his trusty dragon Atlar, he managed to rise to the sky, gainings the Judges' attention, and that's what led to him being endorsed.

...and many other judges not listed here.
